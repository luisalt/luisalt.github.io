Hello,
I'm Luis Alt!

Over the last decade, I’ve been helping people and brands to provide better services. I lead innovation and service design projects, I write bestselling books and articles on the subject, I teach at renowned universities around the globe and I speak at events in portuguese, spanish and english.

I am the founder of Livework in Brazil - a global innovation and service design studio - and EISE - the first School of Service Design in the world. I've also created the first Design Thinking course in Latin America at renowned business school ESPM, and I also teach at Transdisciplinary Design Master at Parsons New School for Design in New York City and at a post-graduate MBA at FAAP in São Paulo. I am the author of Design Thinking Brasil, a best-selling book published by Campus-Elsevier and of the upcoming "Servir Bem para Servir Sempre", a book about the importance of serving for both large and small businesses and how to do it. I speak at events globally in portuguese, spanish and english about Service Design, Design Thinking, Customer Experience, Innovation Metrics and High-Performance Cultures.
